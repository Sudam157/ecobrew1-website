B Corp Certification — In Progress

EcoBrew has applied for B Corp certification, meeting rigorous standards for social and environmental performance, accountability and transparency. Our current impact score: 87.4 / 200 (threshold: 80). Expected certification Q2 2025.

How We Calculate Carbon Savings

Our carbon methodology is based on a lifecycle assessment (LCA) comparing EcoBrew's reusable canister system against equivalent single-use aluminium and cardboard packaging. The LCA was conducted using ISO 14040:2006 standards.

Each stainless steel canister is designed for a minimum of 500 use-cycles. Over its lifetime, the embodied carbon of manufacturing is amortised across all uses, resulting in a per-delivery carbon footprint of approximately 0.08kg CO₂e — compared to 0.89kg CO₂e for single-use equivalents.
